 SoulSync Privacy Policy
Effective Date: May 27, 2025

SoulSync by Chris G ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect
Oura Data (if you connect your Oura Ring): We may access your sleep, activity, and readiness scores via the Oura API.

User Input: If you journal within the app, your entries are stored locally in your browser (via localStorage). We do not collect or transmit your private thoughts.

2. How We Use Information
To provide you with personalized wellness feedback.

To display your daily verse, Oura stats, and journal reflections.

We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Oura API Usage
We use the Oura API to read your wellness scores only if you choose to connect your Oura account. This data is only used within the app to enrich your personal experience.

4. Data Storage
We store all personal journal data locally on your device.

If Oura data is retrieved, it is used in real time and not stored long-term.
